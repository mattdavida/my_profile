My journey...

Hi! Thanks for visiting my page. I am a software developer looking for new opportunities to grow my skills and connect with like minded people. I started learning how to code after I worked as an inside sales representative for a tech company in San Diego, CA. I always wanted to learn, but never really thought it was possible for me until I met a few of the engineers at the company. Once I started to learn, I never looked back. Now I spend my days working on personal projects, trying to climb the ranks on codewars, and just trying to learn everything I can to be a better coder.
